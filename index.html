Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible for energy grants?
Eligibility varies by scheme. Generally, homeowners and tenants on certain benefits, or those with low household income and poor energy efficiency ratings (EPC D or below), may qualify. Our free check covers all current schemes.
Is this check really free?
Yes, completely free with no obligation. We earn a referral fee from approved installers only if you proceed with an installation — you never pay us anything.
How long does the process take?
The eligibility check takes 60 seconds. If you qualify, a specialist will contact you within 24 hours. From survey to installation typically takes 4–8 weeks depending on the scheme.
What happens to my data?
Your data is processed securely and shared only with vetted energy scheme partners to assess your eligibility. We comply with UK GDPR. See our privacy policy for full details.
Do I need to be on benefits to qualify?
Not necessarily. While many schemes prioritise benefit recipients, some — like the Boiler Upgrade Scheme — are open to all homeowners regardless of income. That is why it is worth checking.
